How it started

My name is Jakub, but you can call me kuba, I'm a 20-year-old from Poland and I want to tell you a story about my beginnings in programming.

2019, the beginning of high school, in my computer science class I learned about algorithms and block diagrams, I found it very interesting and spent hours creating new and new diagrams. A few weeks later, I found a C++ programming course on youtube and fell in love with it!

A month later, I saw a poster at my school with the basics of HTML and CSS. After school, I searched for more on the subject and my adventure in web programming began. I spent about six months creating HTML+CSS templates, then it was time to write my first scripts in JavaScript.

Currently, I know a lot about web programming and I am still trying to develop my skills.

In addition to programming, I love to play online games with my friends and have fun with them on discord.

My plans

After graduating from high school, I plan to find a job as a frontend-developer and use my skills for the growth of the company.

In addition, I still want to develop myself and take courses that will help me understand even more about web development.
